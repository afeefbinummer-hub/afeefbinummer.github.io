Customer Shopping Behaviour Analysis

Business Problem

Understand customer buying patterns and purchasing behaviour to identify high-value segments and improve targeting strategies.

What I Did

Cleaned a dataset of 3,900 records using Python (Pandas), performed end-to-end exploratory data analysis, and executed SQL queries to extract actionable business insights. Built an interactive Power BI dashboard to present findings.

Tools Used
Python Pandas SQL Power BI Seaborn
Key Insights
  • Most customers belong to the young and middle-aged groups — the main target segments.
  • Customers show different spending patterns, indicating variations in buying power.
  • Customers with previous purchase history tend to spend more, showing strong loyalty.
  • Product preferences differ across age groups.
Impact

Identified high-value customers and customer trends for better targeting. Loyal customers were found to be 40% more likely to use subscription services.

Global Sales & Profitability Analysis Dashboard

Business Problem

Analyze global sales performance, product profitability, and discount impact across countries to guide pricing and market strategy decisions.

What I Did

Built an end-to-end analytics project using MySQL and Power BI. Created complex SQL queries for KPI analysis, built interactive dashboards with drill-through pages, and modelled $110M in revenue data across 5 countries.

Tools Used
MySQL Power BI Excel DAX Power Query
Key Insights
  • Highest revenue product: Paseo
  • Most efficient product: Amarilla
  • Medium discount strategy delivered best profit margins (14.8%)
  • USA generated the highest sales revenue
  • Overall profit margin: 13.68% across $110M revenue
Impact

Provided leadership with actionable insights on which products and markets to prioritise, and demonstrated that high discounting erodes profitability while medium discounts optimise margins.
